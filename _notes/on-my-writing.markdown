# Writing For My Field



## Goals:
1. I write and publish one blog entry per week. Ideally I am a few weeks ahead, but that's not required
1. I am working on my book about deploying apps and the techniques around that
1. I am participating on twitter daily, targeting hashtags #freecodecamp, #100daysofcode, #CodeNewbie

## Vision:
Craft an image of the job/subfield you want to be in in the next five years. Don't think about the _pay_, think about the _work_.

I want to focus more on backend services and creating robust APIs and database services. I want people to be able to rely on what I've made in order to do their jobs well and provide services in turn for their clients.

I want to learn about API and server architecture, about deployment and robust environments, and about failure and establishing backups and maintaining uptime.
